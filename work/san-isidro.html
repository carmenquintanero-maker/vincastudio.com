Irene Castro is a personal brand inspired by the connection between nature, the sea and the land. These elements represent the essence of the brand and became the foundation of its visual identity.

The goal was to create a visual language that feels calm, elegant and timeless — a system that remains highly recognizable across every application, from stationery to digital presence.
