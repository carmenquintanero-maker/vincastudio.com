About

Small studio.
Big ideas.
Built to make brands clearer.

We help small businesses and visual brands clarify their message, structure their offer and turn that clarity into stronger visual communication. Brands that are easier to understand, easier to trust and harder to ignore.

Our Story

Strategy meets
creative execution.

Vinca Studio was founded by Vincent and Carmen — one side brings the strategy, positioning and business thinking; the other brings the art direction, visual identity and creative execution.

The goal was never simply making brands look better. A well-designed brand that says the wrong thing is still a problem. A clear strategy without a strong visual identity is a missed opportunity.

We built Vinca Studio to do both — and to do them as one thing, not two separate services.

Strategy and design are one discipline. Every visual is a positioning decision.
Why Vinca

A flower
that grows
anywhere.

Vinca is a flower known for resilience and growth. It blooms even in the most difficult conditions — pushing through cracks, covering ground, thriving where other things fail.

The name reflects what we believe about brands: the best ones do not need perfect conditions. They grow because they are clear, consistent and built with purpose.
